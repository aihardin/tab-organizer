Anthropic API Key

Your key is stored locally on this device only and sent directly to api.anthropic.com. It is never shared with any other service. Get a key at console.anthropic.com.

About

Tab Organizer uses Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6) to group your open tabs by topic, identify the most important ones for your daily goal, and generate a markdown summary. Tabs are bookmarked to Chrome and then closed so you can end your day with a clean slate.
